# 📖 Overview

Healthcare information is often difficult for patients to understand. Prescriptions contain abbreviations and medical terminology, laboratory reports include complex clinical values, medicines may interact with one another, and patients frequently struggle to remember medication schedules or identify affordable alternatives. These challenges can lead to medication errors, poor treatment adherence, increased healthcare costs, and confusion regarding personal health.

**Lumi** is an AI-powered multilingual digital health assistant developed to bridge this gap by transforming complex medical information into clear, understandable, and actionable insights.

Powered by **Google Gemini 2.5 Flash**, Lumi enables users to upload prescriptions and laboratory reports, receive AI-generated explanations in plain language, identify drug interactions, compare affordable medication alternatives, organize medical records, and manage medication schedules—all within a single, intuitive platform.

Unlike conventional healthcare applications that focus on a single feature, Lumi integrates document analysis, AI interpretation, multilingual communication, medication management, and secure digital record keeping into one seamless experience, making healthcare information more accessible for patients and caregivers.

---

# 🎯 The Problem Lumi Solves

Healthcare remains information-heavy, yet many patients lack the medical knowledge required to understand their own treatment.

Patients commonly experience problems such as:

* Difficulty understanding prescriptions written using medical abbreviations.
* Inability to interpret laboratory reports without professional assistance.
* Confusion regarding medicine dosage, timing, precautions, and side effects.
* Limited awareness of potentially harmful drug-drug or food-drug interactions.
* High medication costs without knowledge of affordable alternatives.
* Poor medication adherence because of missed doses.
* Medical documents scattered across multiple locations.
* Language barriers preventing patients from understanding healthcare information.

These challenges are particularly significant for elderly patients, caregivers, and individuals managing chronic diseases.

Lumi addresses these issues by acting as an intelligent healthcare companion that simplifies medical information while encouraging users to seek professional medical advice whenever necessary.

# ✨ Core Features

## 📄 AI Prescription Analysis

Users can upload or scan handwritten or printed prescriptions.

Using OCR and Google Gemini Vision capabilities, Lumi extracts relevant medical information and provides:

* Medicine names
* Dosage instructions
* Frequency of administration
* Duration of therapy
* Purpose of each medicine
* Common side effects
* Storage recommendations
* Important precautions

Instead of medical jargon, explanations are generated using patient-friendly language.

---

## 🧪 AI Laboratory Report Interpretation

This helps patients better understand their medical reports before discussing them with healthcare professionals.

# 🧠 Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is the core of Lumi.

The application utilizes **Google Gemini 2.5 Flash** integrated through **Google AI Studio** alongside Vision-based OCR capabilities.

The AI performs several specialized healthcare tasks:

* Prescription understanding
* Laboratory report interpretation
* Medical document summarization
* OCR-based text extraction
* Drug interaction analysis
* Plain-language medical explanations
* Multilingual translation
* Context-aware healthcare conversations

Rather than simply generating responses, the AI analyzes uploaded medical documents, extracts relevant clinical information, interprets findings, and produces structured outputs that are easier for patients to understand.

---

# 🛡 AI System Behavior

Lumi follows carefully designed AI instructions to maximize reliability and patient safety.

The AI is instructed to:

* Act as an educational healthcare assistant.
* Explain healthcare concepts using simple, non-technical language.
* Maintain medically accurate and evidence-based responses.
* Avoid generating fabricated or misleading medical information.
* Clearly communicate uncertainty whenever appropriate.
* Recommend consulting qualified healthcare professionals for diagnosis or treatment decisions.
* Present information in a structured, readable format.
* Support multilingual communication.
* Prioritize patient safety in every response.

The objective is not to replace healthcare professionals but to improve patient understanding and health literacy.

# ⚙ How Lumi Works

The workflow consists of the following steps:

1. Users securely create an account and sign in.
2. A prescription or laboratory report is uploaded or scanned.
3. OCR extracts readable medical text from the uploaded document.
4. Google Gemini AI analyzes the extracted information.
5. Medical terminology is converted into patient-friendly explanations.
6. Users may further explore medication interactions, affordable alternatives, or ask follow-up questions using the AI assistant.
7. Important reports can be securely stored inside the Health Wallet.
8. Medication reminders help users remain adherent to treatment.

This creates a complete AI-assisted healthcare experience from document upload to long-term medication management.
